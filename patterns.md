## Cross-Cutting Patterns

### Repetition Patterns
- **Repetition and Insistence** (ch55): Angular patterns sound natural if repeated persistently
- **Mutation of Clones** (ch28): One-change-per-copy variation
- **Mutation Over Generations** (ch29): Sequential mutation chain

### Constraint Patterns
- **Arbitrary Constraints** (ch06): Self-imposed limitations
- **One Part at a Time** (ch09): Focus constraint
- **Rendering as Commitment** (ch70): Technical constraint

### Variation Patterns
- **Mutation of Clones** (ch28): Parallel mutation
- **Mutation Over Generations** (ch29): Sequential mutation
- **Note Transformations** (ch30): Systematic note changes
- **Fuzzy Boundaries** (ch27): Partial section changes

### Humanization Patterns
- **Partial Quantization** (ch35): Partial grid alignment
- **Ghost Notes** (ch37): Quiet anticipatory hits
- **Behind/Ahead of Beat** (ch35): Micro timing shifts
- **Humanizing With Automation** (ch57): Envelope/tuning variation
- **TBR Thinking** (ch38): Physical human restrictions
